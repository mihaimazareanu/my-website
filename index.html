Hi there.

Welcome to my website.

My name is Mihai and I live in the beautiful city of Hamburg, Germany.

I went to the International Computer School in Constanta, Romania, after which I decided to study economics so I did a Bachelor Degree in International Business at the Ovidius University in Constanta, Romania.

I also studied for one semester at the International Business School in Jönköping, Sweden.

For 18+ years I have built my career in the transport and logistics industry, where I started as a Dispatcher and evolved to Assistant Operations Manager and then Haulier Procurement Manager.

However, after so much time in transport & logistics I noticed that I don't enjoy working in this business anymore, therefore I decided it was time for a career change. So I decided to take coding, which was just a hobby, to a professional level. Therefore I decided to join the Web Development Bootcamp from neue fische.

If you want to know more about my professional career, you can take a look at my CV.
